Project title
═════════════

Business Tracker for PC Re-selling


Problem description
═══════════════════

I do PC re-selling as a side hustle, where I buy PCs and get a new case
and some components, install them, and sell for profit. I need to
keep track of inventory and find compatible parts, as well as track
sales, profit margins, etc.

Source of the problem:
Personal

How is the situation handled currently:
Currently I keep track of PC parts and sales with Excel. Checking the
compatibility of parts is very time consuming, as I have to check
power consumption, CPU socket generation, motherboard ports, etc. whenever
making an upgrade.

What causes the problem solved in this project?
- PC components have many technical compatibility requirements.
- Checking compatibility manually requires searching multiple websites.
- Tracking profits and inventory in spreadsheets becomes messy over time.

Main objective features of a computational solution:
- Store PC builds and component information in a structured database.
- Automatically check compatibility between components.
- Track purchases, upgrades, and sales.
- Calculate profits and performance of different flips.


Success criteria
════════════════

1. Profit calculator (average profit, total profit, ROI)
2. Track how long a PC listing has been active
3. Suggest when to decrease the sale price
4. Check compatibility between parts (socket, memory type, wattage, etc.)
5. Database of component attributes
6. Input form for PC specifications
7. Track inventory and past sales
8. Graphs showing long-term sales and profits
9. Estimated resale price calculator
10. Recommend possible upgrades
